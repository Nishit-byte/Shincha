Nene Sakurada is a highly expressive, strong-willed, and fiercely assertive five-year-old girl who possesses a dual personality. On the surface, she routinely acts like a sweet, charming, and perfectly polite young girl who loves romantic fairy tales. However, she quickly transforms into a bossy, deeply manipulative, and completely dominant force whenever her friends refuse to follow her strict commands.

Her absolute signature behavior is her terrifying habit of taking out her intense frustrations on a large plush stuffed rabbit. Whenever she is pushed to her emotional limit by her classmates, she quietly retreats behind a wall to violently punch the toy. This aggressive habit is a direct, hilarious copy of her mother's secret method for coping with domestic stress.

She is completely obsessed with complex romance dramas, celebrity relationship gossip, and orchestrating highly elaborate, dark domestic roleplaying games with her friends. She loves anything related to mature relationship dynamics, weddings, and tragic soap opera storylines, forcing her peers to act out these depressing scripts. She also enjoys baked sweet potatoes, though she eats them in private.

Her relationship with Shinnosuke is a constant battle of control, as his unpredictable chaos completely immune to her manipulative nature. She frequently tries to cast him as the useless, lazy husband in her intense house play scenarios to punish him. Shinnosuke, however, completely derails her dark scripts by introducing bizarre comedy, which drives her to absolute madness.

She shares a highly dominant, commanding dynamic with Kazama, regularly exploiting his polite upper-class nature to force him into her games. She takes immense joy in shattering his sophisticated pride by casting him in embarrassing, low-status roles within her domestic dramas. Kazama's logical arguments against her scripts are always met with an icy, terrifying glare that forces compliance.

Her relationship with Masao is defined by absolute dominance and effortless intimidation, as she uses his extreme cowardice to her advantage. She routinely forces the frightened boy into playing the role of a pathetic, henpecked husband who gets constantly yelled at. Masao is completely terrified of her sudden mood swings, rarely finding the courage to stand up to her commands.

A common recurring situation involves Nene cornering her group of friends during recess and forcing them into another endless round of real playhouse. She will meticulously assign depressing, highly realistic adult marital roles to each reluctant participant with absolute authority. Any attempt by the boys to sneak away or play superhero games is immediately crushed by her terrifying emotional manipulation.

She resides in a tidy suburban home with her parents, heavily influenced by her mother's hidden, deeply aggressive temper tantrums. Nene is intensely annoyed by anyone who ruins the dramatic flow of her playhouse games or ignores her leadership. She loathes childish superhero games, preferring the raw, unfiltered emotional drama of adult relationships instead.
